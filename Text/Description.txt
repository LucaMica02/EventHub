EventHub is an application with the aim of simplifying the organization, management and the search for events. A user can register as a private user or as an event creator.
Private users can search for available events, register for events and give feedback on the events they have attended.
Creators can create new events and manage events already created, creators also have a ranking based on the feedback received that goes from 1 to 5.
Events can take place online or in person, in the first case the url to connect to must be specified, otherwise it will be necessary to give the address of the event.
